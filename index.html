About

idqfxnymre, a 15-year-old producer from Bangladesh, has been making waves in the music scene with his unique blend of raw rhythm, funk, and phonks. Drawing inspiration from artists like Eternxlkz and Ogryzek, he’s spent over two years crafting sounds that push boundaries and explore new sonic territories. His music is defined by deep basslines and unsettling rhythms, creating an eerie, energetic vibe that grabs attention. As a professional video editor, idqfxnymre combines his audiovisual talents to enhance the experience of his music, providing a fully immersive journey for his listeners. His funks are bold and experimental, igniting intense energy while maintaining a distinct, haunting quality. With each track, he challenges the limits of traditional genres, offering something fresh and unpredictable. Dive into his world of bold, resonant funks—but be warned, these sounds can be both captivating and chilling.
